## 🧠 Who I Am

I design and build **production-grade AI agent systems** that connect LLM reasoning to live business data. My work sits at the intersection of business systems architecture, multi-agent AI, and cloud infrastructure.

I do not build prototypes. I build systems that are observable, cost-governed, client-isolated, and built to scale from Day 1.

Currently specialising in **multi-agent architectures** where domain-specific agents share a common infrastructure layer, each owning one slice of the business and feeding a collective intelligence layer above them.

Based in **Nairobi, Kenya**. Building for Africa and the world.

## 🎯 Engineering Principles
01  Build systems, not isolated scripts
02  Design for observability from Day 1
03  Cost governance is an engineering concern, not an afterthought
04  Document decisions and trade-offs as you build, not after
05  Client data isolation is non-negotiable in multi-tenant systems
06  Test the deterministic parts. Evaluate the non-deterministic parts.
07  Surface blockers the same day with a proposed solution already attached
